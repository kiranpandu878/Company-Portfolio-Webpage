IDEA

Coming up with ideas is easy, but finding the right one takes clarity. At Phantez, we work with you to understand the problem, current process, and your goals. All shared data stays confidential as we align our solutions with your vision.              

BLUEPRINT

Once we understand your strengths and gaps, we define the right solution. We then blueprint the structure, strategy, and tech needed—step by step—to meet your goals.                 

DESIGN

The next step is to custom-design a solution tailored to your goals and requirements. The team will brainstorm, position the design, focusing on usability, simplicity. The outcome could be a new website or a social media marketing plan, all built around a clean and creative design.

DEVELOPMENT

Once you approve the design, it’s handed to development for coding and back-end setup. We create a wireframe or social media plan and conduct pre-launch testing to ensure your goals are met. Our UI/UX team focuses on optimizing a marketing strategy to maximize your market impact.

TESTING

This is the client’s first real look at the solution. After development, test the live greyscale version on test servers for instant feedback. The tech team ensures fast performance, while designers remain ready to make adjustments to meet client goals.                                                       

DELIVER

Once you are satisfied with the project, and only then, will we hand you the keys. We don’t just give you the keys and dust it off, we will be with you in unleashing your new strategy and reporting the progress, while continually optimizing to ensure your success.
